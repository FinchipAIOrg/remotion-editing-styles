# American Vintage Color-Block

Use bold rounded color fields, slab-like display type, badges, seals, and nostalgic advertising structure.

## Editorial behavior

- Cut rhythm: punchy and campaign-like; land claims on clear visual beats.
- Footage: use centered crops, product close-ups, and decisive before/after comparisons.
- B-roll: packaging, signage, labels, product detail, archive-inspired imagery.
- Transitions: rounded block pushes, badge stamps, and clean hard cuts.
- Sound: stamp hits, short brass-like stabs, claps, or warm percussive impacts.

## Visual system

- Deep purple: `#22005A`.
- Teal: `#0FA6B8`.
- Cream: `#FFF4D4`.
- Orange-red: `#E95E3E`.
- Green: `#3F9C5A`.
- Type: heavy vintage display or slab-serif for English; bold display Chinese with strong counters.
- Motif: circular seal or product badge.

## Caption system

Use centered, oversized statements for takeovers and simpler lower-third captions over footage. Emphasize keywords with cream or orange capsules.

## Avoid

Cyber grids, chrome, luxury minimalism, thin editorial serif text, low-contrast muted palettes, and badge overload.
