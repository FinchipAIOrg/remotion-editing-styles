# Cyber Brutalism

Use black structure, restrained neon-lime accents, Swiss-grid hierarchy, oversized numerals, and precise interface-like overlays.

## Editorial behavior

- Cut rhythm: fast opening, structured body, deliberate pauses on proof.
- Footage: use clean punch-ins and split layouts with screenshots or metrics.
- B-roll: interfaces, charts, code, diagrams, devices, and evidence captures.
- Transitions: hard cuts, line reveals, grid wipes, and numbered section takeovers.
- Sound: precise clicks, digital ticks, low impacts, and controlled risers.

## Visual system

- Background: `#0A0A0A`.
- Accent: neon lime `#B8FF2C`.
- Text: white `#F5F5F5`.
- Secondary: gray `#8B8B8B`.
- Type: bold geometric sans for headlines and monospaced labels.
- Motif: HUD brackets, progress rail, or one rounded smiley.

## Caption system

Place captions in thin outlined boxes or a stable lower-third rail. Highlight only the active phrase in lime. Keep the grid and scan treatments away from faces.

## Avoid

Dense face-covering scanlines, illegible microtext, generic dashboard clutter, glossy neon gradients, cute sticker clouds, and nonstop glitch effects.
