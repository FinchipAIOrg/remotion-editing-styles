# Style Selection

Choose a style from content and audience, not from novelty.

| Preset | Best fit | Editorial energy | Avoid when |
|---|---|---|---|
| Natural retro geometric | Design, culture, brand, aesthetic education | Bold, composed, organic | The message needs softness, cuteness, or technical precision |
| Xiaohongshu illustrated poster | Lifestyle, beauty, food, travel, youth commerce | Playful, friendly, buoyant | The topic is formal, severe, or data-heavy |
| Relaxed handwritten poster | Personal opinion, creator commentary, emotional storytelling | Human, spontaneous, imperfect | The user expects strict corporate polish or dense structured data |
| American vintage color-block | Product, campaign, course, packaging, strong CTA | Punchy, nostalgic, promotional | The message needs quiet minimalism or delicate illustration |
| Cyber brutalism | AI, software, productivity, startup, research, data | Precise, high-contrast, urgent | The message needs warmth, craft, or childlike friendliness |

## Recommendation procedure

Score each candidate from 0–2 on:

1. Topic fit.
2. Audience fit.
3. Platform fit.
4. Emotional fit.
5. Asset fit.

Recommend the highest score and explain the decisive factor. If two styles tie, choose the one that better supports comprehension.

## Mixing rule

Use a dominant style plus no more than two traits from one secondary style.

Good:

- Cyber brutalism structure with American-vintage badge shapes.
- Natural-retro typography with restrained handwritten annotations.

Bad:

- Combining every palette, font family, motif, and transition.
- Switching presets scene-by-scene without a narrative reason.

Record any mix explicitly:

```text
Dominant: Cyber brutalism
Borrowed traits: American-vintage rounded badge, warm cream CTA
Preserved invariants: black/lime hierarchy, Swiss grid, precise cut rhythm
```

## Default format

When the request is a vertical talking-head edit and no platform is given, use 1080×1920 at 30 fps as a provisional default. Confirm or revise it when source metadata or delivery requirements are known.
