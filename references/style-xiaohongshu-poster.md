# Xiaohongshu Illustrated Poster

Use playful low-fidelity illustration, rounded display text, soft high-saturation color, and handmade commercial energy.

## Editorial behavior

- Cut rhythm: lively but friendly; use short reaction holds.
- Footage: preserve the speaker and frame stickers around face-safe space.
- B-roll: lifestyle details, product close-ups, lists, screenshots, and simple demonstrations.
- Transitions: poster-page slides, sticker pops, and quick card flips.
- Sound: soft pops, clicks, and light percussive accents.

## Visual system

- Card: cream `#FFF3D8`.
- Pink: `#F79BB7`.
- Blue: `#3BAFE3`.
- Green: `#65D46E`.
- Highlight: lemon `#F4E84A`.
- Outline: brown `#6B4A2F`.
- Type: rounded or handwritten Chinese display face with a legible sans fallback.
- Motif: one small mascot reused consistently.

## Caption system

Use short rounded caption chunks. Alternate two main colors only when it improves phrase grouping. Pair major keywords with one relevant sticker, not a cloud of unrelated icons.

## Avoid

Futuristic HUDs, dense data grids, photoreal 3D stickers, sterile corporate layouts, tiny labels, and decorations covering the subject.
