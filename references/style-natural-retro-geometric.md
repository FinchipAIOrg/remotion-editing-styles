# Natural Retro Geometric

Use a 1970s-inspired modular system: large geometric type, organic flat color fields, and restrained high-impact composition.

## Editorial behavior

- Cut rhythm: confident medium pace; hold hero typography long enough to read.
- Footage: preserve A-roll and use punch-ins at claim changes.
- B-roll: favor objects, print texture, architecture, packaging, and tactile details.
- Transitions: block wipes, modular assembly, and hard graphic cuts.
- Sound: muted thumps, paper taps, or short tonal pulses.

## Visual system

- Base: pale yellow-green `#DEE9BF`.
- Primary: grass green `#4C9953`.
- Contrast: brick red `#BC4937`.
- Highlight: warm yellow `#FDCA57`.
- Text: near-black `#111111`.
- Type: ultra-bold rounded geometric Chinese sans with a condensed display sans fallback.
- Motif: dot array or one abstract modular mark.

## Caption system

Use heavy, compact captions with generous surrounding space. Emphasize at most one phrase per caption using a reversed block or warm accent.

## Avoid

Glossy gradients, glass effects, photoreal decorative graphics, thin type, soft handwriting, and constant bouncing motion.
